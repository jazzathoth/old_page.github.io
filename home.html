I am building this site as both a portfolio, as a sort of repository for my projects, and in some instances to provide information for others working on similar projects. ARM Projects

ARM Projects Main Page

I started learning ARM Cortex development on Cortex M3 chips made by ST Micro. Arm Cortex microcontrollers are a more modern and more complex microcontroller architecture than AVR,PIC, or many of the other common microcontrollers available at non-commercial prices. The Cortex M3 is a 32 bit microcontroller available from numerous manufacturers often with a full System-On-Chip like array of peripherals.
